What we do best is help you feel your best

Here at Healthy Minds we do mental health for the 21st Century. By putting you in contact with a professional therapist, psychiatrist, and or psychologist.

We create the connections you want and need

After you answer a survey to have a basic profile diagnosis, we provide those answers to the doctors and therapists who have decided to treat patients with similar profiles as yours. After one of them accepts to be your designated health worker, you can scheduele a video call or simply have a one on one text chat with your therapist.

We remind you that you're not alone

We provide a space for group chats to be created, and you will be assigned to one according to your therapist. This group will serve as a support group for you and for the other memebers just like you. You will help others facing similar issues and they will help you too, as one big community!

We keep your data safe and private

The only time your data is in a server is when answering the initial survey, Afterwards, we delete your answers and all data provided to your doctor or therapist is only sent to your doctor or therapist. We don't hold it in a server. We believe that your medical data should be private and only shared to those you want to share it with. This is also to prevent it from being malliciously used by any third party intermedium. In other words, your data is your data.

We do it all for free

We believe that anyone deserves to be heard, deserves to love themselves and deserve to have a healthy, happy life. These are trying times for everyone, not everyone who needs or deserves this kind of treatment can afford it. That's why we decided to make this as affordable as can be: $0.00 USD. Mental health should be a right, and that's what we want to provide, that is what we do. We give you the right to free mental health assistance.

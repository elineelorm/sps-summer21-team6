A brighter affordable future for mental health

We envision a more modern take on therapy, where anyone of any status can recieve the therapy they need to live a more fullfiling life.

Mental Health is a right, not a privilege

We believe that this is one of the many steps forward in making mental health a right. Article 25 of the Universal Declaration of Human rights already establishes that everyone has the right to health and well being, but psychiatry and psychology are stil very expensive in developed and developing countries. We envision a future where everyone can truly live their right to mental health.

A World of Emotional Responsibility and Maturity

We hope to live in a world where every adult has more control over how the handle and express their emotions. People need to understand that everyone can feel different things, and expressing feelings of joy, sadness, anger, envy, and every other emotion on the specter. It's not about what emotions you feel, but how you deal with them that matters. This is our vision, our creed.
